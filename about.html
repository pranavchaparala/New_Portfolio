ABOUT | Profile | NYC

Designer

I'm a multidisciplinary designer with an MFA in Design and Technology from Parsons, exploring the intersection of design and technology, with expertise in product design, industrial design, and creative technology. My work focuses on creating tangible and meaningful experiences — turning complex data and systems into something simple and intuitive. I actively build with AI — using tools like Claude, Cursor, and Google AI Studio to prototype and build my ideas and design interactions that feel intentional.

My notable work includes OxygenOS 12 at OnePlus, reaching 16–20 million global users, and the Luna Ring at Noise — a smart ring app I designed from scratch that won a Red Dot Award in 2024 and sold out in 3 hours. I am a Merit Scholar at Parsons and have been selected as a student speaker for Design Days by the Interaction Design Foundation.
